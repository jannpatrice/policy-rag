# POL-006 Expense Reimbursement Policy

**Document ID:** POL-006
**Document Title:** Expense Reimbursement Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Finance Department

---

# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish guidelines for the reimbursement of reasonable and necessary business expenses incurred by employees while conducting company business.

InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. is committed to reimbursing legitimate business expenses in a timely and consistent manner.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Regular employees
* Probationary employees
* Managers
* Executives

Contractors and consultants may be subject to separate contractual arrangements.

---

# 3. Guiding Principles

Reimbursable expenses must be:

* Business-related
* Necessary
* Reasonable in amount
* Properly documented
* Approved according to company requirements

Employees should spend company funds as responsibly as they would spend their own.

---

# 4. General Requirements

To qualify for reimbursement:

* The expense must support a legitimate business purpose.
* The expense must comply with this policy.
* Appropriate receipts or supporting documentation must be submitted.
* Required approvals must be obtained.

Failure to meet these requirements may result in denial of reimbursement.

---

# 5. Submission Timeline

Employees must submit reimbursement requests within thirty (30) calendar days of the expense date.

Requests submitted after thirty (30) days may be denied unless an exception is approved by Finance.

---

# 6. Reimbursement Processing

Approved reimbursements are typically processed within two payroll cycles following approval.

Processing times may vary depending on:

* Documentation completeness
* Approval delays
* Payroll schedules

---

# 7. Required Documentation

Employees must provide:

* Official receipts
* Invoices
* Booking confirmations
* Proof of payment

Documentation should clearly indicate:

* Vendor name
* Date
* Amount
* Description of expense

Finance may request additional supporting documentation when necessary.

---

# 8. Meals and Entertainment

## Internal Meetings

Reasonable meal expenses incurred during approved business meetings may be reimbursed.

## Client Meetings

Meals with clients may be reimbursed when there is a legitimate business purpose.

Employees should document:

* Client name
* Meeting purpose
* Attendees

## Meal Limits

The standard reimbursement limit is:

* ₱800 per person for domestic business meals

Amounts exceeding this limit require manager justification.

---

# 9. Transportation Expenses

The following transportation expenses may be reimbursed:

* Taxi
* Grab
* Ride-hailing services
* Public transportation
* Parking fees
* Toll fees

Transportation should be reasonable and cost-effective.

Employees should select practical transportation options whenever possible.

---

# 10. Air Travel

Air travel must be approved before booking.

Employees should generally book:

* Economy Class for domestic flights
* Economy Class for international flights under six (6) hours

Business Class requires executive approval.

Travel requirements are further detailed in POL-007 Travel Policy.

---

# 11. Accommodation Expenses

Hotel expenses incurred for approved business travel may be reimbursed.

Employees should select accommodations that are:

* Safe
* Reasonably priced
* Convenient to business activities

Luxury accommodations are generally not reimbursable unless specifically approved.

---

# 12. Communication Expenses

Business-related communication expenses may be reimbursed including:

* Mobile calls
* Temporary internet access
* Roaming charges during approved travel

Supporting documentation is required.

---

# 13. Training and Certification Expenses

Approved training expenses may be reimbursed including:

* Certification examinations
* Course fees
* Training materials

Additional requirements are outlined in POL-011 Learning and Development Policy.

---

# 14. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses are generally not reimbursable:

* Traffic violations
* Parking penalties
* Personal entertainment
* Alcoholic beverages
* Personal shopping
* Family travel expenses
* Personal subscriptions

Finance may deny reimbursement for expenses deemed excessive or unrelated to business activities.

---

# 15. Approval Authority

## Employee Expenses up to ₱5,000

Approval required:

* Immediate Manager

## Expenses Above ₱5,000 up to ₱20,000

Approvals required:

* Immediate Manager
* Department Head

## Expenses Above ₱20,000

Approvals required:

* Immediate Manager
* Department Head
* Finance Manager

---

# 16. Corporate Credit Cards

Authorized employees issued corporate credit cards must:

* Use cards only for approved business purposes.
* Submit supporting documentation promptly.
* Reconcile expenses according to Finance requirements.

Personal use of corporate cards is strictly prohibited.

---

# 17. Foreign Currency Expenses

International business expenses incurred in foreign currencies may be reimbursed.

Reimbursement shall be converted to Philippine Pesos (PHP) using the exchange rate applied by the company's financial institution at the time of processing.

Supporting documentation must include original currency amounts.

---

# 18. Fraud and Misrepresentation

Employees may not:

* Submit duplicate claims
* Alter receipts
* Misrepresent business purposes
* Claim personal expenses as business expenses

Fraudulent reimbursement requests may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment.

---

# 19. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require written approval from:

* Finance Manager
* Relevant Department Head

Approved exceptions must be documented.
