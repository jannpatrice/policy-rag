# POL-010 Recruitment and Hiring Policy

**Document ID:** POL-010
**Document Title:** Recruitment and Hiring Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture Department

---

# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to establish a fair, consistent, and effective recruitment process that enables InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. to attract, assess, and hire qualified talent.

The company is committed to merit-based hiring and equal employment opportunities.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all recruitment and hiring activities conducted by the company.

It covers:

* Full-time employees
* Probationary employees
* Interns
* Project-based employees

---

# 3. Hiring Principles

The company seeks to:

* Hire the best-qualified candidates.
* Promote diversity of experience and thought.
* Maintain fairness and consistency.
* Comply with applicable employment laws.
* Deliver a positive candidate experience.

---

# 4. Workforce Planning

Department Heads are responsible for identifying staffing requirements.

Hiring requests should include:

* Business justification
* Proposed role
* Budget availability
* Required skills and experience

All hiring requests require approval before recruitment activities begin.

---

# 5. Job Requisitions

Open positions must be documented through an approved job requisition process.

A requisition should include:

* Job title
* Reporting structure
* Employment type
* Compensation range
* Required qualifications

---

# 6. Recruitment Channels

Candidates may be sourced through:

* Employee referrals
* Job boards
* Professional networks
* Company website
* University partnerships
* Recruitment agencies

The company may use multiple channels simultaneously.

---

# 7. Equal Employment Opportunity

Hiring decisions shall be based on:

* Skills
* Qualifications
* Experience
* Role requirements
* Interview performance

The company does not discriminate based on protected characteristics as defined by applicable laws.

---

# 8. Candidate Screening

People & Culture may conduct an initial screening to evaluate:

* Relevant experience
* Communication skills
* Compensation expectations
* Availability
* Eligibility to work

Candidates who meet minimum requirements may proceed to interviews.

---

# 9. Interview Process

The standard hiring process generally includes:

## Stage 1: HR Screening

Initial qualification review.

## Stage 2: Hiring Manager Interview

Assessment of role suitability.

## Stage 3: Technical or Functional Assessment

Evaluation of job-specific capabilities when applicable.

## Stage 4: Final Interview

Assessment of organizational fit and overall readiness.

The company may modify interview stages based on role requirements.

---

# 10. Interview Responsibilities

Interviewers are expected to:

* Conduct fair evaluations.
* Follow approved interview guidelines.
* Maintain confidentiality.
* Avoid discriminatory questions.
* Document assessment results.

---

# 11. Candidate Assessments

The company may require:

* Technical examinations
* Case studies
* Presentations
* Written exercises
* Practical demonstrations

Assessment requirements vary by role.

---

# 12. Background Verification

The company may conduct background verification before finalizing employment.

Verification may include:

* Employment history
* Educational credentials
* Professional certifications
* Reference checks

Employment offers may be withdrawn when material misrepresentations are discovered.

---

# 13. Reference Checks

Reference checks may be conducted for shortlisted candidates.

Candidates may be asked to provide professional references.

Information gathered shall be treated confidentially.

---

# 14. Employment Offers

Employment offers must be approved by:

* Hiring Manager
* Department Head
* People & Culture

Offers should be communicated in writing.

Only authorized representatives may extend employment offers.

---

# 15. Offer Acceptance

Candidates should confirm acceptance within five (5) business days unless otherwise specified.

Extensions may be granted at management discretion.

---

# 16. Pre-Employment Requirements

New hires may be required to submit:

* Government-issued identification
* Tax documentation
* Employment records
* Educational records
* Government benefit information

Additional documentation may be requested when necessary.

---

# 17. Employee Referrals

The company encourages employee referrals.

Referral bonuses may be offered for selected positions.

To qualify:

* The referring employee must identify the candidate before recruitment engagement.
* The candidate must successfully complete probation.

Referral bonus amounts may be adjusted by management.

---

# 18. Recruitment Confidentiality

Recruitment information must be handled confidentially.

Employees involved in hiring may not:

* Share interview feedback inappropriately.
* Disclose candidate information.
* Discuss confidential compensation information.

---

# 19. Internal Candidates

Qualified employees may apply for internal opportunities.

Internal candidates shall be evaluated fairly and consistently.

Business needs and performance history may be considered.

---

# 20. Policy Violations

Examples of violations include:

* Falsification of candidate information
* Unauthorized employment offers
* Discriminatory hiring practices
* Breach of candidate confidentiality

Violations may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 21. Exceptions

Exceptions require approval from:

* Head of People & Culture
* Relevant Department Head

All approved exceptions must be documented.
