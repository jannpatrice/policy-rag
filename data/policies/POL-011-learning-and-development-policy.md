# POL-011 Learning and Development Policy

**Document ID:** POL-011
**Document Title:** Learning and Development Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture Department

---

# 1. Purpose

InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. is committed to fostering continuous learning and professional development.

This policy establishes guidelines for employee training, certifications, educational assistance, professional development activities, and learning support programs.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all regular and probationary employees.

Certain benefits may require minimum tenure or additional approvals.

---

# 3. Learning Philosophy

The company believes that continuous learning:

* Improves employee effectiveness
* Supports career growth
* Increases client value
* Strengthens organizational capabilities
* Encourages innovation

Employees are expected to take ownership of their professional development.

---

# 4. Eligible Learning Activities

Examples of eligible development activities include:

* Professional certifications
* Technical training courses
* Leadership programs
* Conferences
* Workshops
* Industry seminars
* Academic programs

Approval requirements vary based on cost and business relevance.

---

# 5. Annual Learning Budget

Regular employees are eligible for an annual learning budget of up to ₱30,000 per calendar year.

The budget may be used for approved:

* Training courses
* Certification examinations
* Professional memberships
* Learning materials

Unused budgets do not carry over to the following year.

---

# 6. Certification Support

The company encourages employees to pursue certifications relevant to their role.

Examples include:

* Microsoft Certifications
* AWS Certifications
* Google Cloud Certifications
* Project Management Certifications
* Cybersecurity Certifications

Other certifications may be approved when aligned with business objectives.

---

# 7. Certification Reimbursement

Employees may request reimbursement for certification examination fees when:

* The certification is pre-approved.
* The employee passes the examination.
* Required documentation is submitted.

Employees who fail an examination may request reimbursement approval at management discretion.

---

# 8. Study Leave

Employees may be granted Study Leave for approved certification examinations or educational activities.

Standard Study Leave allowance:

* Up to three (3) working days per calendar year

Additional leave may be approved for exceptional circumstances.

Study Leave is separate from Vacation Leave.

---

# 9. Conferences and Events

Employees may attend conferences, workshops, and industry events when approved by management.

Approval considerations include:

* Business value
* Cost
* Relevance to role
* Knowledge-sharing opportunities

Travel-related expenses are governed by POL-007 Travel Policy.

---

# 10. Internal Knowledge Sharing

Employees are encouraged to share knowledge gained through:

* Presentations
* Brown bag sessions
* Workshops
* Internal documentation
* Communities of practice

Management may require knowledge-sharing activities as part of sponsored development programs.

---

# 11. Academic Programs

The company may support approved academic programs including:

* Graduate degrees
* Professional diplomas
* Specialized educational programs

Support may include:

* Flexible scheduling
* Study Leave
* Partial reimbursement

Approval is not guaranteed and depends on business requirements and budget availability.

---

# 12. Professional Memberships

The company may reimburse approved professional memberships when they provide meaningful business value.

Examples include:

* Professional associations
* Industry organizations
* Technical communities

Memberships require manager approval.

---

# 13. Learning Commitments

Employees receiving company-sponsored learning support are expected to:

* Complete approved programs.
* Participate actively.
* Apply acquired knowledge.
* Share relevant learnings when requested.

---

# 14. Development Plans

Employees may work with managers to establish development plans that align with:

* Career goals
* Business objectives
* Skill requirements
* Performance expectations

Development plans may be reviewed during performance discussions.

---

# 15. Priority Skills

The company may prioritize investment in skills that support strategic objectives including:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Cloud Computing
* Software Architecture
* Cybersecurity
* Data Engineering
* Project Management
* Leadership

Priority areas may change over time.

---

# 16. Training During Working Hours

Approved training may be completed during working hours when:

* Manager approval is obtained.
* Business obligations remain satisfied.
* Client commitments are not negatively impacted.

---

# 17. Reimbursement Documentation

Employees requesting reimbursement must provide:

* Proof of payment
* Completion certificates when applicable
* Examination results when applicable
* Supporting receipts

Requests should be submitted within thirty (30) calendar days of completion.

---

# 18. Employee Separation

Employees who voluntarily resign within ninety (90) calendar days of receiving company-funded training exceeding ₱20,000 may be required to reimburse a prorated portion of training expenses.

The company may waive recovery requirements at its discretion.

---

# 19. Policy Violations

Examples of violations include:

* Submission of fraudulent reimbursement requests
* Falsification of training records
* Misuse of development funds

Violations may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 20. Exceptions

Exceptions require approval from:

* Department Head
* Head of People & Culture

All approved exceptions must be documented.
