# POL-001 Employee Handbook

**Document ID:** POL-001
**Document Title:** Employee Handbook
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** People & Culture Department

---

# 1. Purpose

This Employee Handbook provides employees of InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. with an overview of the company's mission, values, employment practices, workplace expectations, and employee benefits.

The handbook serves as a guide and should be read in conjunction with all other company policies.

---

# 2. About InterCloud Solutions

InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. is a technology consulting and software delivery company specializing in:

* Artificial Intelligence Solutions
* Cloud Engineering
* Enterprise Integration
* Custom Software Development
* Managed Services

The company serves clients across the Philippines, Singapore, Australia, and the United States0.

---

# 3. Mission

To help organizations solve complex business challenges through innovative software, cloud, and AI solutions.

---

# 4. Core Values

## Client Success

We prioritize customer outcomes and long-term partnerships.

## Continuous Learning

We encourage employees to continuously improve their technical and professional skills.

## Ownership

Employees are expected to take responsibility for commitments and deliverables.

## Collaboration

We work as one team regardless of location, department, or seniority.

## Integrity

We conduct business honestly and ethically.

---

# 5. Employment Classifications

Employees may be classified as:

## Regular Employee

Employees who have successfully completed their probationary period.

## Probationary Employee

Employees undergoing an evaluation period of up to six (6) months.

## Project-Based Employee

Employees hired for a specific project or duration.

## Intern

Students participating in internship or practicum programs.

---

# 6. Working Hours

The standard workweek consists of forty (40) hours.

Typical business hours are:

* Monday to Friday
* 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM Philippine Time

Alternative schedules may be approved by department heads.

Employees are expected to maintain availability during agreed working hours.

---

# 7. Remote Work Philosophy

InterCloud Solutions operates under a remote-first model.

Employees may perform their duties remotely unless:

* Client requirements require onsite work.
* Company events require attendance.
* Managers determine a business need for in-person collaboration.

Additional rules are detailed in POL-003 Remote Work Policy.

---

# 8. Attendance Expectations

Employees are expected to:

* Attend required meetings.
* Notify managers of absences promptly.
* Maintain accurate attendance records.
* Be responsive through approved communication channels during working hours.

Repeated attendance issues may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 9. Compensation

Employee compensation is determined by:

* Job responsibilities
* Experience
* Market benchmarks
* Individual performance

Salary payments are processed twice monthly.

All compensation is paid in Philippine Pesos (PHP).

---

# 10. Employee Benefits

Eligible employees may receive:

* Government-mandated benefits
* 13th Month Pay
* Vacation Leave
* Sick Leave
* Learning and Development Support
* Internet Allowance
* Performance-Based Incentives

Specific eligibility requirements are described in the applicable policies.

# 11. Government Benefits

The company complies with all applicable Philippine labor laws.

Eligible employees receive contributions to:

* Social Security System (SSS)
* PhilHealth
* PAG-IBIG Fund

Mandatory deductions are processed in accordance with government regulations.

# 14. Workplace Conduct

Employees must:

* Treat others respectfully.
* Avoid harassment and discrimination.
* Protect company information.
* Follow all company policies.

---

# 15. Information Security

Employees are responsible for protecting:

* Client information
* Company intellectual property
* Employee records
* Business systems

Security requirements are detailed in POL-004 Information Security Policy.

---

# 16. Use of Company Assets

Company-issued equipment remains the property of InterCloud Solutions.

Employees are expected to:

* Maintain equipment responsibly.
* Prevent unauthorized access.
* Return assets upon separation.

Additional requirements are described in POL-008 Equipment & Asset Policy.

---

# 17. Resignation

Employees are expected to provide at least thirty (30) calendar days written notice before their intended separation date unless otherwise required by law or approved by management.

The company reserves the right to shorten or waive the notice period when appropriate.

---

# 18. Policy Compliance

Employees are responsible for understanding and complying with all company policies.

Failure to comply may result in corrective action up to and including termination of employment.

---

# 19. Handbook Updates

InterCloud Solutions reserves the right to revise this handbook at any time to reflect operational, legal, or business requirements.

Employees will be notified of material changes.
