# POL-008 Equipment and Asset Policy

**Document ID:** POL-008
**Document Title:** Equipment and Asset Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Operations Department

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for the issuance, use, maintenance, protection, and return of company-owned equipment and assets.

InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc. provides employees with equipment necessary to perform their duties effectively while protecting company investments and client information.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

* Employees
* Contractors issued company assets
* Interns
* Consultants authorized to use company equipment

---

# 3. Company-Owned Assets

Company assets may include:

* Laptops
* Monitors
* Docking stations
* Mobile devices
* Security tokens
* Peripherals
* Software licenses
* Office equipment

All company-issued assets remain the property of InterCloud Solutions.

---

# 4. Equipment Issuance

Eligible employees may be issued equipment based on their role and business requirements.

Typical equipment packages may include:

## Standard Employee Package

* Company laptop
* Laptop charger
* Security software
* Collaboration tools

## Technical Employee Package

* Company laptop
* Additional monitor
* Development tools
* Security software

Additional equipment may be provided with management approval.

---

# 5. Asset Tracking

All company-issued assets shall be recorded in the company's asset inventory system.

Records may include:

* Asset tag number
* Device serial number
* Assigned employee
* Issue date
* Return date

Employees are responsible for assets assigned to them.

---

# 6. Employee Responsibilities

Employees must:

* Protect company equipment from damage.
* Prevent unauthorized use.
* Maintain physical security.
* Report issues promptly.
* Follow security requirements.

Employees should exercise reasonable care when handling company assets.

---

# 7. Acceptable Use

Company assets must be used primarily for legitimate business purposes.

Limited personal use is permitted provided such use:

* Does not interfere with work responsibilities.
* Does not violate company policies.
* Does not create security risks.

Additional requirements are outlined in POL-005 Acceptable Use Policy.

---

# 8. Equipment Security

Employees must:

* Lock devices when unattended.
* Use approved authentication methods.
* Enable required security controls.
* Protect devices during travel.

Devices should never be left unattended in unsecured public locations.

---

# 9. Remote Work Equipment

Employees working remotely are responsible for maintaining a secure environment for company assets.

Employees should:

* Prevent access by household members.
* Secure equipment when not in use.
* Protect confidential information.

Remote work requirements are further detailed in POL-003 Remote Work Policy.

---

# 10. Loss or Theft

Employees must report lost or stolen equipment immediately.

Reports should include:

* Asset description
* Last known location
* Date and time of discovery
* Circumstances surrounding the incident

Employees should report losses within twenty-four (24) hours of discovery.

Information Security may initiate remote lock or wipe procedures when appropriate.

---

# 11. Damage to Equipment

Accidental damage should be reported promptly.

Employees should not attempt unauthorized repairs.

The company will assess repair or replacement requirements on a case-by-case basis.

Repeated negligence may result in corrective action.

---

# 12. Maintenance and Updates

Employees must allow required:

* Security updates
* Operating system updates
* Device management updates
* Software maintenance activities

Employees may not disable required security controls.

---

# 13. Software Licensing

Software provided by the company may only be used for authorized business purposes.

Employees may not:

* Share software licenses
* Copy licensed software
* Install software on unauthorized devices

All software usage must comply with vendor licensing agreements.

---

# 14. Equipment Replacement

Equipment may be replaced when:

* Hardware failure occurs
* Business requirements change
* Devices reach end-of-life status
* Security requirements necessitate replacement

Replacement decisions are subject to company approval.

---

# 15. Travel with Company Equipment

Employees traveling with company assets must:

* Maintain physical possession whenever practical.
* Avoid checked baggage when carrying laptops.
* Protect devices from theft.
* Follow security requirements.

International travel may involve additional security requirements.

---

# 16. Asset Audits

The company may conduct periodic asset audits.

Employees must cooperate with audit activities including:

* Device verification
* Asset inspections
* Inventory confirmations

Failure to participate may result in investigation.

---

# 17. Separation from Employment

Employees must return all company assets before or on their final working day unless otherwise directed.

Required items may include:

* Laptops
* Chargers
* Monitors
* Mobile devices
* Security tokens
* Access cards

Final clearance may be delayed until all assets are returned.

---

# 18. Unreturned Assets

The company reserves the right to pursue recovery of company property through appropriate legal and administrative means.

Employees should promptly coordinate with Operations regarding any return issues.

---

# 19. Asset Transfers

Employees transferring between departments may be required to:

* Return existing equipment
* Exchange equipment
* Complete inventory updates

All transfers must be documented.

---

# 20. Policy Violations

Examples of violations include:

* Unauthorized asset transfers
* Failure to report loss or theft
* Intentional damage
* Unauthorized software installation
* Sharing company equipment without approval

Violations may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 21. Exceptions

Exceptions require written approval from:

* Operations Manager
* Information Security Manager

All approved exceptions must be documented.
