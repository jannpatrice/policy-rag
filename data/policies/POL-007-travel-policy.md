# POL-007 Travel Policy

**Document ID:** POL-007
**Document Title:** Travel Policy
**Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Owner:** Operations Department

---

# 1. Purpose

This policy establishes guidelines for business travel conducted on behalf of InterCloud Solutions Philippines, Inc.

The objective is to ensure that travel is necessary, cost-effective, safe, and aligned with business objectives.

---

# 2. Scope

This policy applies to all employees traveling for approved business purposes.

Business travel may include:

* Client meetings
* Project workshops
* Training programs
* Conferences
* Internal company events
* Strategic planning sessions

---

# 3. Travel Principles

Employees are expected to:

* Travel only when business objectives cannot reasonably be achieved remotely.
* Select cost-effective options.
* Prioritize employee safety.
* Comply with all company policies.

Travel should deliver measurable business value.

---

# 4. Travel Authorization

All business travel requires prior approval.

## Domestic Travel

Requires approval from:

* Immediate Manager

## International Travel

Requires approval from:

* Immediate Manager
* Department Head

International travel exceeding seven (7) calendar days additionally requires executive approval.

---

# 5. Travel Booking

Employees should use company-approved booking channels whenever possible.

Travel arrangements should be booked early to minimize costs.

Employees should not make non-refundable bookings without appropriate approvals.

---

# 6. Air Travel

## Domestic Flights

Employees should book Economy Class airfare.

## International Flights

Employees should book Economy Class airfare.

Business Class travel is permitted only when:

* Approved by an Executive
* Required for documented medical reasons
* Required by client contract

---

# 7. Ground Transportation

Approved transportation options include:

* Grab
* Taxi
* Ride-hailing services
* Public transportation
* Rental vehicles when approved

Employees should choose practical and cost-effective transportation options.

Luxury transportation services are generally not reimbursable.

---

# 8. Accommodation

Employees may book hotels that are:

* Safe
* Professionally suitable
* Reasonably priced
* Conveniently located

## Domestic Accommodation Guideline

Maximum recommended rate:

* ₱6,000 per night

## International Accommodation Guideline

Maximum recommended rate:

* USD 200 per night

Higher-cost accommodations require justification and approval.

---

# 9. Per Diem Allowance

The company may provide daily meal allowances during approved business travel.

## Domestic Travel

Meal allowance:

* ₱1,500 per day

## International Travel

Meal allowance:

* USD 50 per day

Employees may be required to provide supporting documentation when requested.

---

# 10. Conference Attendance

Employees attending conferences should:

* Obtain prior approval
* Participate in relevant sessions
* Share learnings with colleagues when appropriate

Conference expenses may be reimbursed subject to approval requirements.

---

# 11. Client-Sponsored Travel

When clients cover travel expenses, employees must comply with client policies where applicable.

Employees should still adhere to company standards of conduct and professionalism.

---

# 12. Travel Safety

Employees are expected to exercise reasonable judgment while traveling.

Employees should:

* Follow local laws
* Protect company assets
* Secure confidential information
* Maintain emergency contact information

---

# 13. International Travel Requirements

Employees traveling internationally must ensure:

* Passport validity of at least six months
* Required visas and travel authorizations
* Compliance with destination-country regulations

Employees are responsible for submitting required documentation before departure.

---

# 14. Combining Personal and Business Travel

Employees may request approval to combine personal travel with business travel.

Conditions include:

* No additional cost to the company
* Clear separation of personal and business expenses
* Prior management approval

The company will reimburse only business-related expenses.

---

# 15. Travel Advances

Travel advances may be requested for approved business travel.

Requests should be submitted at least five (5) working days before departure.

Employees must reconcile travel advances within ten (10) working days after returning.

---

# 16. Expense Reimbursement

Travel-related expenses must comply with POL-006 Expense Reimbursement Policy.

Employees are responsible for:

* Collecting receipts
* Maintaining records
* Submitting expenses on time

Failure to provide documentation may result in reimbursement denial.

---

# 17. Cancellation of Travel

Employees should notify management promptly when travel plans change.

Unused tickets, reservations, and travel advances should be addressed immediately.

The company reserves the right to recover unnecessary costs resulting from avoidable cancellations.

---

# 18. Conduct During Travel

Employees represent InterCloud Solutions while traveling for business.

Employees are expected to:

* Behave professionally
* Respect cultural norms
* Follow client expectations
* Protect company reputation

Misconduct during business travel may result in disciplinary action.

---

# 19. Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require written approval from:

* Department Head
* Operations Director

All approved exceptions must be documented.
